Engineering a seamless web-based platform that enabled patients to: 📅 Self-schedule appointments online 📈 Track their visit history and upcoming consultations 🌟 Access custom experiences based on their premium or regular membership tier I contributed across the product lifecycle — from MVP planning and technical architecture to feature implementation and deployment — showcasing a balanced blend of product ownership and engineering execution.

To extend the app’s impact, I facilitated its transition to another clinic, where it was later adapted to new requirements.
